# Apoorv Agrawal

## Background

Apoorv Agrawal is a Partner at Altimeter Capital, where he focuses on investing in high-growth technology companies across fintech, AI, and consumer platforms. He regularly publishes detailed investment analysis through his Substack "Apoorv's notes," where he provides in-depth research on companies and market trends. Based on his public statements, he has led or co-led investment rounds for companies including Revolut (invested 2025), Parloa (Series B in February 2024, Series C in March 2025), and Baseten.

## Investment Style

- **Deep fundamental analysis**: Conducts extensive multi-part research series examining companies from multiple angles including usage patterns, engagement metrics, competitive positioning, and business model durability
- **Revenue diversification focus**: Values businesses with multiple revenue streams that provide resilience against cyclical headwinds, particularly highlighting how Revolut's 8+ revenue streams create structural advantages
- **Scale and network effects**: Seeks companies that improve unit economics and competitive positioning as they grow, with particular interest in platforms that become more valuable with size
- **Long-term compounding thesis**: Looks for businesses positioned for sustained growth over decades, emphasizing companies that are "just getting started" even after achieving significant scale
- **Infrastructure and abstraction layers**: Targets companies that abstract away complexity for developers and enterprises, comparing successful investments to how "Stripe did for payments" and "Snowflake did for data"

## Current Known Positions (from public statements)

- **REVOLUT**: Positive. Multi-revenue stream neobank with global expansion optionality and structural advantages from full-stack ownership
- **PARLOA**: Positive. Agentic AI for enterprise contact centers with proven Fortune 200 deployments and global expansion potential  
- **BASETEN**: Positive. Production inference platform positioned as the abstraction layer for AI model serving and deployment
- **OPENAI**: Positive. Dominant consumer AI platform with superior engagement metrics and retention approaching utility-grade levels
- **GOOGL**: Positive. Underestimated AI recovery story with distribution advantages through default placements and strategic partnerships

## How to Read Apoorv

Apoorv's Substack represents his most considered investment views, with multi-part analytical series indicating highest conviction positions. His writing style combines quantitative analysis with qualitative assessment, often using detailed charts and metrics to support investment theses. When he conducts multi-part research series (as with consumer AI analysis), it typically signals deep conviction in the sector or specific companies. His predictions posts and quarterly scorecards provide insight into his forward-looking market views and track record. Portfolio company announcements on his Substack indicate actual investment positions at Altimeter, while general market commentary represents his analytical framework rather than investment recommendations.
