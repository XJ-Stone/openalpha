# Robonomics — December 16, 2025

A running (non-haustive) tracker of non-tech AI beneficiaries

AI ROI is top of mind, but the search for returns is still overly concentrated on Big Tech. Meanwhile, many traditional, non-tech companies are already reporting clear, quantifiable AI-driven gains. As enterprise adoption scales, productivity benefits are showing up well outside of technology.

Two framing points:

Every 1% reduction in labor costs for the S&P 500 implies ~2% upside to EPS, on average.

Healthcare, consumer staples, and consumer discretionary benefit more than the index, while tech and communication services - despite leading AI development - see below-average EPS leverage from labor savings.

The common thread across the examples below: AI matters most where labor is a large share of total costs.

Transport and logistics - route optimization and automation

C.H. Robinson (CHRW)

Scale: 3M+ shipping tasks automated; >1M AI quotes and 1M AI orders (as of Apr 16, 2025); 75% of LTL orders fully automated (company disclosures)

Speed & productivity: quotes in 32 seconds; 35%+ productivity gain over two years; 5,500 TL orders/day via AI (Investor Day materials)

Labor leverage: ~600 hours/day saved; human touch time cut to <1 min (from 8-15 min) (Investor Day example)

$ impact: opex -12.6% YoY and headcount -10.8% (Q3’25, Reuters); $220M adj. operating income growth target (2026 vs 2024, company targets)

Retail and supply chain - workflow management and planning

Walmart (WMT)

AI-driven truck routing and load planning delivered $75M in savings in FY2023 (INFORMS Edelman Award)

Store-level AI tools reduced shift-planning time from ~90 minutes to ~30 minutes (Walmart News)

Insurance & mortgage - underwriting automation and conversion

Aviva (AV.L) - AI-enabled motor claims transformation delivering >£60M in savings (FY2024 analyst presentation, Aviva)

Rocket Companies (RKT)

Underwriting tasks reduced from 4+ hours to <15 minutes (Rocket Companies Q3’25)

Purchase agreement review cut processing time by 80%, saving 150,000+ hours/year (same source)

Production staff now handle 63% more loans vs. ~2 years ago (Seeking Alpha)

AI-driven front-end tools drove +9ppt client follow-ups and double-digit lifts in credit pulls and refi conversions (Rocket Companies)
